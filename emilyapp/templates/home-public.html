Emily App is a simple web and mobile app for tracking breastfeeding, pumping, and diaper changes.

Log in »

Mobile access

Emily App is completely optimized for all mobile phones and tablets. Whether you've got an iPhone, Android, Blackberry, or iPad, the app will look and work great on your device.

Quick controls

Instant buttons for "Feed Left", "Feed Right", "Pee", "Poop", "Pump Left", and "Pump Right". Because when you're up at 4am, the last thing you should need is a pen and paper.

Multiple children

Have a few kids? How about twins? We've got you covered, with a quick way to switch between children. Even if your children are years apart, Emily App will save you time.

You tell us!

The developer of Emily App has a newborn, too. So we're constantly building new features that matter for real parents. Need a feature? Email Nick!
